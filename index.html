Introduction

Knitting is the process of creating loops of yarn and then interlocking them to create a fabric. It's a great way to make your own clothes and accessories. Knitting is a fun and relaxing hobby that can be done anywhere, anytime. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's always something new to learn in the world of knitting.

Information

What you'll need:

  • Yarn
  • Knitting needles
  • Scissors
  • Tape measure
  • Stitch markers

Basic knitting stitches:

Stitch Description
Stockinette Knit one row, purl one row
Garter stitch Knit every row
2 x 2 Ribbing Knit two, purl two over a multiple of 4 stitches every row
